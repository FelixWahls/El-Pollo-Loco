How to play

or

Use the right arrow-key on your Keyboard or the right arrow on the bottom of the Screen (only on mobile) to let Pepe walk to the right.

or

Use the left arrow-key on your Keyboard or the left arrow on the bottom of the Screen (only on mobile) to let Pepe walk to the left.

or

Use the Spacebar on your Keyboard or the up arrow on the bottom of the Screen (only on mobile) to let Pepe jump into the air.

or

Use the D-key on your Keyboard or the throw button on the bottom of the Screen (only on mobile) to let Pepe throw a Flask into the direction he is currently facing.
